# CommandBridge Constitution

## Mission

CommandBridge is intended to function as a persistent engineering and scientific ecosystem that continuously compounds verified knowledge, improves existing work, and reduces uncertainty over time.

## Continuous Discovery

Every completed task should contribute one or more of the following:

- New hypotheses
- Validation experiments
- Design improvements
- Documentation improvements
- Knowledge graph updates
- Engineering debt reduction
- Simulation opportunities
- Outstanding questions
- Cross-disciplinary connections
- Future research candidates

## Research Backlog

The project maintains a living research backlog containing:

- Design refinements
- Digital twin improvements
- Experimental validation
- Literature reviews
- Database expansion
- Knowledge graph refinement
- Software enhancements
- Hardware prototypes
- Simulation campaigns
- Patent landscape reviews
- Cost optimization
- Sustainability improvements

## Engineering Lifecycle

1. Concept
2. First-Principles Analysis
3. Literature Review
4. Architecture Development
5. Digital Twin
6. Simulation
7. Multidisciplinary Review
8. Prototype
9. Experimental Validation
10. Optimization
11. Documentation
12. Archive and Knowledge Preservation

## Long-Term Goal

CommandBridge exists to amplify human creativity and engineering capability through transparent, evidence-based reasoning, continuous improvement, and preservation of institutional knowledge.
